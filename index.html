How it works

Voice Demos is a four-track recorder for layering musical ideas — like an old cassette four-track, but in your pocket.

Recording your first track
Tap a track's round button on the left until it shows R (record). Hit the big record button, then ▶ play to start. Tap stop when done.

Track states
Each round button cycles through four modes: R record · P play · S solo · M mute. Tap it to cycle.

Layering (overdubbing)
Set your recorded track to P, set an empty track to R, then record again. You'll hear the first track while you add the new one — they stay in sync.

Punch-in
Drag any waveform left/right to move the playhead, then record to replace just that section.

Tempo, count-in & FX
Use the pills above the transport to set tempo (with optional metronome), toggle a spoken count-in, or add tape-style saturation.

Exporting
Tap Export to download a stereo mix or each track as a separate WAV file.

Tip: headphones give the cleanest overdubs, but the app compensates for timing automatically so speakers work too.

Sync & Timing
Timing is adjusted automatically for your device — you shouldn't need to change anything. If overdubs still land slightly late or early, switch to Manual and nudge until layered tracks lock up.
